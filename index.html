Ansher Capital -

Your global partner in cross border deals

Ansher Capital, a subsidiary of Ansher Investments LLP (UK), is the most recognized and awarded investment bank in Uzbekistan. Established in 1999, the firm has built a solid reputation for excellence across regional and international markets. Over the years, Ansher Capital has received more than ten prestigious local and international awards, consistently being named the Best Investment Bank in Uzbekistan and Central Asia. We advise leading enterprises and financial institutions in Uzbekistan on capital raising, investment structuring, and strategic transactions. Our team combines global best practices with in-depth local market knowledge to deliver tailored solutions that create long-term value for our clients
