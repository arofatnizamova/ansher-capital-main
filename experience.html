Abboskhon Rasulev

Mr. Rasulev is serving as the Chief Executive Officer of Ansher Capital LLC, an Investment Banking arm of Ansher Investments LLP since 2023. Before his position as CEO, he was the Senior Managing Director (TPM) (2022 - 2023), Managing Director (Trading Operations) (2020 - 2022), and Managing Director for consultancy services (2020) in Ansher Capital LLC. 

He holds B.A. (Hons) degree from Westminster International University in Tashkent.

His mother tongue is the Uzbek language. Mr. Rasulev is fluent in Russian and English.
